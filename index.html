Usajobs Search Tool

Have you ever looked for jobs on usajobs.gov? If you have, you may have noticed that the page is hard to read on mobile devices. Also, not all job results are relevant--for example, searching for mathematician often returns jobs like waiter or bartender.

Hence, I made a web app for searching for US federal government jobs. It is cross platform and mobile friendly, and it uses machine learning to sort jobs from most to least interesting. You can try it here or check out the code.
